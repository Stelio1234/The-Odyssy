How the song ties to the story

The line "My shadow's the only one that walks beside me" shows Odysseus strugle and loneliness of being a leader. In the lyrics "I walk a lonely road" and "Sometimes I wish someone out there will find me" Odysseus feels loneliness during his journey and a disconnection with society. Being a leader, he has his crew's responsibility on his shoulders, but he feels that he faces many of these struggles completely alone, as in many decisions like navigating between Scylla and Charybdis or the cyclops.
